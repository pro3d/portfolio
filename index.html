About

History of Pro3d:

Pro3d.com is the first domains I purchased for my first business, JTM Professional 3D, which I started back in 1998 with aspirations for a career in 3d animation. That career only lasted a few years with a Rapid Prototyping company called Hunter Hawk, in Concord, CA. I soon fell in love with web design & development. This site was launched to showcase my works and I would like to continue using it for that reason, even if it's not all 3D content. For all the 3d artists that stop in... I'll find more time for 3D soon, and post up my projects, thanks for all the emails and comments.
